NEWS FOR CANVAS USERS

Spring Canvas Courses Conclude on Sunday, June 23

On June 23, Spring 2019 Canvas courses will conclude and convert to read-only mode. Teachers will not be able to add new content, add people, or edit grades after this date. To extend active access to your Spring courses, or to get more information about restricting student access to courses, submitting final grades or accessing past courses, please see our End-of-Quarter Canvas Tips for Teachers.

Summer 2019 Canvas Courses Available

If you don’t see your Summer 2019 courses on your Canvas dashboard, please let the Canvas team know at canvashelp@stanford.edu

  • Post your syllabus
  • Copy content from a past course
  • Request an appointment to get help with setting up your Canvas course

Instructor Tips

  • Remember to post your syllabus
  • Import your content from last year
  • Find your past courses with the new “View All Courses” button on the dashboard
